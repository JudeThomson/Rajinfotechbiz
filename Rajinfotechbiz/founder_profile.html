Founder and Managing Director Profile

R.Wilfred Raju

MCA, M.Sc, PGDCA, Six Sigma Black Belt, ITILV3 (Foundation),
with a Global Business Leadership certification from U21 GU, Singapore.

Wilfred serves as the Managing Director of Raj InfotechBiz Solutions Pvt. Ltd., a company committed to enhancing organizational performance through innovative solutions especially in Healthcare IT. With over 35 years of comprehensive experience, including more than 20 years in IT delivery management, Wilfred has established himself as a leader in the industry.


As a seasoned professional, Wilfred has held various leadership roles such as Vice President, Delivery Head, Principal Consultant, and Program Manager in esteemed organizations like OptiRisk India Pvt. Ltd., Tech Mahindra (Satyam Computer Services Ltd), NatureSoft, and Geosoft, among others. Throughout his career, he has successfully managed and delivered over 40 software projects spanning a wide array of industries including healthcare, logistics, retail, shipping, and supply chain management.


Wilfred's notable contributions include the development of award-winning healthcare IT software such as Raj PIS (Patient Information System) and Doctor 1 Teleclinic, aimed at revolutionizing patient care and hospital management. His expertise extends to strategic consulting, where he has provided business and technology solutions to Fortune 500 companies, mid-sized enterprises, and small businesses, resulting in significant cost reductions and increased productivity.


Wilfred and his team are developing an AI-based decision support system integrated with robotics to aid doctors and consultants in primary healthcare consultations. Their project aims to bridge urban-rural gaps and enable Remote Patient Monitoring, Home/Tele ICU services, Teleclinic operations, as well as provide healthcare services directly to senior citizens at their doorsteps and through home healthcare initiatives.


Recognized for his strategic thinking and leadership prowess, Wilfred has mentored numerous next-generation leaders and conducted various workshops and training programs on entrepreneurship, Six Sigma, business process re-engineering, and IT portfolio rationalization. He is also a published author, speaker, and recipient of the prestigious Trivandrum Healthcare Leadership Award.


Wilfred's passion for innovation and commitment to excellence continue to drive Raj InfotechBiz Solutions towards new heights of success, making him a trusted advisor and visionary leader in the industry.
