RAJ RETAIL POS


Raj Retail POS Overview:

Raj Retail POS is an Android based mobile App. for Traders, Dealers, Agencies, Distributors, Shops, Stores etc.

Trading made easy. Do trading/business through Raj Retail POS, an innovative android based one stop solution for Tablet users. It replaces use of Computers, reduces total cost and increases productivity.

Features of Raj Retail POS

Financial Management

  • Cash Outstanding
  • Cash Receipt
  • Expenses Report
  • Sales invoice with print

Stock and Inventory Management

  • Opening Stock
  • Closing Stock
  • Reorder Stock
  • Stock with expiry date
  • Stock Adjustments

Order and Purchase Management

  • Order Taking
  • GRN (Goods Receipt Note)
  • Purchase/Production details etc.
  • Rate Fixation

Reporting and Printing

    Printing of reports through Bluetooth printer etc.
